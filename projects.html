Project 1

CTU-buddy is a dynamic website designed to enhance the student experience at Cape Town University. Offering a centralized platform, it provides quick access to essential academic resources, personalized dashboards for efficient organization, and collaborative spaces for shared learning. The website streamlines event management, ensuring students stay informed about campus activities. With a focus on user-friendly functionality, CTU-buddy aims to simplify administrative tasks, making student life more convenient and productive. Elevate your academic journey with CTU-buddy, where seamless navigation meets enhanced efficiency.
